Our Story

Three Generations of Healthcare Excellence

In 2012, with the emergence of the Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan (DRAP) Act, PDH Healthcare was formally established — continuing the P.D.H Group's 75-year legacy of pharmaceutical excellence.

With a heritage spanning three generations exclusively within P.D.H Healthcare, the company epitomizes a legacy steeped in hard work, unwavering integrity, and an unrelenting commitment to quality.

PDH Healthcare specializes in nutraceuticals, cosmeceuticals, OTC and alternative healthcare products — delivering safe, effective, and consumer-focused solutions nationwide.

Our History

A Legacy Built Over Seven Decades

Following Pakistan's independence in 1947, P.D.H was established in 1948 as the country's first healthcare manufacturing company — receiving the first local manufacturing license from the Government of Pakistan.

Since then, P.D.H has grown alongside Pakistan, contributing consistently to the nation's healthcare development through regulated, high-quality production across multiple specialized units.

Pakistan's First 3 Generations DRAP Compliant
1947
Independence Pakistan Established

Pakistan achieved independence and emerged as a sovereign nation, creating the need for local healthcare infrastructure.

1948
Foundation P.D.H Group Founded

Established as Pakistan's first healthcare manufacturing company at 42 Nicholson Road, Lahore — receiving the country's first local manufacturing license.

1970
Expansion P.D.H Laboratories

Second manufacturing unit established on Sheikhupura Road, Lahore, specializing in injectable formulations.

1981
Recognition No. 1 National Healthcare Company

P.D.H was officially awarded the status of Pakistan's Number One National Healthcare Company.

1990
Growth P.D.H Pharmaceuticals

Third manufacturing unit launched at Ferozepur Road, Lahore, specializing in tablets and syrups.

2012
Milestone PDH Healthcare Launched

With DRAP Act 2012 establishing formal regulation for OTC and alternative healthcare, PDH Healthcare was officially launched — continuing the group's legacy as a pioneer in regulated consumer healthcare.

Visionary Leadership Driving Quality & Growth

PDH Healthcare was envisioned and founded by Mr. Jameel Altaf, a qualified Pharmacist with over 25 years of executive leadership experience across pharmaceutical manufacturing, marketing, and operations within the P.D.H Group.

Having previously served in senior leadership roles at P.D.H Pharmaceuticals and P.D.H Laboratories, Mr. Altaf brings deep industry knowledge, regulatory understanding, and strategic direction to PDH Healthcare.

His vision is to build a globally competitive consumer healthcare company, rooted in Pakistan's legacy of pharmaceutical excellence and aligned with modern regulatory standards. Under his leadership, PDH Healthcare continues to strengthen its position through quality-driven products, professional governance, and a consumer-centric approach.
