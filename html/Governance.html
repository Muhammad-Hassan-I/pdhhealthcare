Board Committees

To assist the Board in carrying out its responsibilities effectively and transparently

Audit Committee

The Audit Committee assists the Board in fulfilling its financial management and related reporting responsibilities for PDH, including compliance with laws and regulations.

HR & Remuneration

Responsible for assisting the Board in selection, compensation and retention of top talent and key positions including their development and succession planning.

Finance Committee

The committee assists the Board with respect to oversight of the company's Financial Management and Resources, evaluating specific financial strategy initiatives.

Nomination & Governance

Provides assistance in ensuring appropriate structure, size and balance of skills at PDH Boards and their Committees, identifying qualified individuals to become directors.
